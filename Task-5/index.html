Aladdin

A charming street-smart boy discovers a magical lamp that grants wishes, leading him on an adventure to win the heart of Princess Jasmine and defeat the evil Jafar.

Frozen

When Queen Elsa’s icy powers trap her kingdom in eternal winter, her fearless sister Anna embarks on a magical journey to bring back summer and mend their bond.

Jungle Book

Raised by wolves in the wild, young Mowgli embarks on a thrilling adventure of self-discovery with the help of his animal friends Baloo and Bagheera while escaping the fierce tiger Sher Khan.

Moana

A brave island girl sets sail across the ocean to restore the heart of a goddess and save her people, discovering her true destiny along the way.

Mufasa

The wise and noble king of the Pride Lands, Mufasa guides and protects his son Simba, teaching him about responsibility, courage, and the circle of life.

Snow White

A kind and gentle princess escapes the jealousy of her wicked stepmother and finds refuge with seven dwarfs, discovering love and hope along the way.

Tangled

Rapunzel, a spirited princess with magical long hair, escapes her tower with the help of charming thief Flynn Rider, embarking on a fun-filled adventure to discover the world and her true identity.

Wish

A young girl makes a heartfelt wish on a star, setting off a magical adventure where she learns about hope, courage, and the power of believing in herself.

Encanto

In a magical Colombian house, the extraordinary Madrigal family each has a unique gift—except Mirabel, who must discover her own strength to save the magic that surrounds them.

Little Mermaid

Ariel, a curious mermaid princess, dreams of living on land and falls in love with a human prince, embarking on a daring journey to follow her heart.
